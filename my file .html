Naruto Uzumaki

Story

The story is about Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who wants to become the leader of his village, the Hokage. The story is told in two parts: Naruto's pre-teen years and his teenage years. Themes The anime highlights the importance of friendship and loyalty through the bonds between the characters. It also inspires viewers that hard work and love can help you reach your goals. Characters Naruto is boisterous, exuberant, and unorthodox. He ends his sentences with "Dattebayo!" when he's emotional. Sasuke Uchiha is Naruto's rival who tries to gain power to avenge his clan. Creator Masashi Kishimoto created the manga series, which was serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump. The anime adaptation began broadcasting in 2002
